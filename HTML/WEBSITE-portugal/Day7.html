Day 7: 7 Cities in 7 Days (M:5/27)

This is the day I make my way back to Lisbon. Not to leave back to the US, though. Still have one more town to explore. This day is to travel all the way from the North of Portugal back to Lisbon. The rest of the day is dedicated to exploring Lisbon even further, but this time focusing on the Alcantara and Belem districts near the river! I plan to see the Mosteiro dos Jeronimos, the Torre de Belem, and the National Museum of Archeology.
