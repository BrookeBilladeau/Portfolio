Day 8: The Town I'm most excited For! (T:5/28)

After my stay over in Lisbon, I hop a train for the mountain town of Evora. This town is described as a visitor having time traveled back into medieval days with how well the buildings have been tended to and restored! It's not as crowded as some of the other towns I'm visiting along the way, but it's still a popular tourist destination. I plan for this whole city to be my exploration grounds! The hostels here a slightly pricier, but in my opinion, will be worth it!
